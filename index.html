Moonflower's Coffee Shop

Menu

We offer a variety of coffee and espresso beverages, as well as a selection of fresh bakery items. Our selection of scones and muffins changes seasonally so that we may use the freshest ingredients. We always have brownies, our special house chocolate and strawberry cream cakes.

Locations

Our main shop is located on the corner of Main and Gridania Ave. We are open Monday - Friday from 8am - 8PM.
